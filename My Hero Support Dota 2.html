Undying

Undying is a tanky support hero with abilities that can turn team fights in his team's favor. He is often played as a lane support to secure farm for his carry and create opportunities for kills.

Shadow Shaman is a versatile support with a strong crowd control and pushing abilities. He excels in controlling enemies and securing objectives.

Venomancer

Venomancer is a versatile hero that can be played as a support or core. His playstyle involves dealing damage over time and controlling the pace of the game.
