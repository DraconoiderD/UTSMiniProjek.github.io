Rank Herald

Herald is the lowest rank in Dota 2. Each Herald rank is divided into five levels (I-V). Herald players are generally still learning the basics of the game.

Rank Guardian

The Guardian is the next rank after the Herald. The Guardian includes players who have a basic understanding of the game.

Rank Crusader

Crusader is a middle rank that includes players with a better understanding of the game.

Rank Archon

Archon is a rank that includes players with a higher level of skill. Archon players often have a good understanding of the game's meta and tactics.

Rank Legend

Legend is a rank that includes players who have solid skills. Legend players often have a good understanding of game tactics and strategies.

Rank Ancient

Ancient is a rank that covers players with a high level of skill. Ancient players usually have an in-depth understanding of various aspects of the game.

Rank Divine

Divine is a rank that includes players who are highly skilled in the game. Divine players are the most skilled players and may have played Dota 2 for a long time.

Rank Immortal

Immortal is the highest rank in Dota 2. This is an exclusive rank given only to the best players in the world.
